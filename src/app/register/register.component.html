Welcome to YAPP APLICATION!

This is a software for the management of your clinic, where you can store and manage data of your patients, appointments, reports, treatments. You can also register users (workers of your clinic), where they will have their own personal space to manage their work.

With your "administrator user" profile, you can see how your workers work.

Your workers will be able to see their tasks, but not those of other workers.

Once registered, a confirmation email will be sent to you. You will need to validate the email to access your account.
